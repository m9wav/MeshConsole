Flood Advertisement

Send a flooded advertisement from a MeshCore device. The advertisement will propagate through the mesh network via all reachable repeaters.

About Flood Advertisements

A flood advertisement broadcasts your node's presence across the entire mesh network with a 255-hop limit.

  • Propagates through all reachable repeaters
  • Updates your node info on remote devices
  • Helps establish routing paths to your node
